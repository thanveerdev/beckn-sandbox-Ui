About Beckn Protocol

Beckn is an open network protocol that enables location-aware, local commerce across industries through an interoperable network. This sandbox demonstrates how different domains (mobility, retail, healthcare, etc.) can use the same standardized protocol for discovery and transactions.

How It Works

  • Search: Find services/products across domains
  • Select: Choose specific items or services
  • Init: Initialize order with customer details
  • Confirm: Finalize the transaction
  • Track: Monitor order progress
